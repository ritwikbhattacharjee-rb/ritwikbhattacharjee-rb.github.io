Profile

My communications practice is built at the intersection of scholarship, strategy and public audiences.

My work focuses on translating specialized knowledge into communications that are clear, timely and useful to the people they need to reach. I have led and supported communications across UBC units, including work in thought leadership, public programming, faculty storytelling, event communications, newsletters, social media, websites, design and photography.

I bring an editorial eye, a strategist’s sense of audience, and an academic background that helps me work confidently with complex research, sensitive public issues and institutional priorities.

How I Work

My approach to communications.

01

Translate complexity
