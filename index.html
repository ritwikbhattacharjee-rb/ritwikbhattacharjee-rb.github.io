Featured Communications Initiative

Arts Perspectives on AI

Building public-facing thought leadership on artificial intelligence

I developed and led the Arts Perspectives on AI editorial series, identifying interdisciplinary story opportunities, interviewing faculty experts, writing every published feature and managing the editorial process from concept to publication.

The series positioned UBC Arts faculty and alumni as public voices on the societal implications of artificial intelligence across journalism, music, Indigenous data stewardship, linguistics, higher education and public policy.
